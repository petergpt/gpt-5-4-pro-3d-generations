# Angkor Wat at Sunrise

Model: GPT-5.4-Pro Extended

## Prompt

```text
Create the iconic Angkor Wat temple at sunrise with reflection pools.

If you use Three.js, add an import map (before the module script) mapping "three" and "three/addons/" to the same pinned version, and import only via those names. Never reuse identifiers in the same scope - use descriptive variable names.

#### THE TEMPLE

Angkor Wat's distinctive silhouette:
- Five lotus-bud towers (the central one tallest)
- Elaborate Khmer architecture
- Long causeway leading to the entrance
- Gallery walls with carved details
- Weathered grey stone with patches of lichen/moss
- Scale: the largest religious monument in the world

#### THE REFLECTION

The famous view across the reflecting pools:
- Rectangular pools (the "moat") in the foreground
- Mirror-perfect reflection of the temple
- Lotus flowers floating on the water
- The doubled image creating symmetry

#### THE SUNRISE

The visual centerpiece:
- Sun rising directly behind the central tower
- Sky transitioning from deep blue to orange/pink
- The temple silhouetted then gradually illuminated
- Golden light catching the carved surfaces
- Mist rising from the jungle

#### ATMOSPHERE

- Ancient, spiritual, mysterious
- The jungle pressing in around the edges
- Birds (perhaps egrets) taking flight
- The stillness of dawn
- A thousand years of history

#### CONTROLS

- Time slider (pre-dawn silhouette -> sunrise -> morning light)
- Mist level
- Camera: Classic reflection view, Causeway approach, Aerial, Close-up details
- UI compact and collapsible

#### TECHNICAL

- Planar reflection for water
- Target >=55 FPS
- Clamp devicePixelRatio <= 2
```
